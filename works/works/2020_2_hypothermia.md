---
title: Hypothermia for bass drum
tags: solo
desc: Gran cassa and electronics
date: 2020
---
The piece utilizes a feedback system, where a speaker is placed close
to the resonating skin of the drum and two microphones are placed
close to the batter skin of the drum. In this piece I also use
software I wrote to mimic the behaviour of the Japanese mouth-organ
shō, called <a href="https://github.com/mattiashallsten/mh-sho"
target="_blank">MHSho</a>.

Premiered at Lilla Salen, Kungl. Musikhögskolan 14/4 -- 2021.

Ivar Koij - gran cassa  
Mattias Hållsten - electronics
