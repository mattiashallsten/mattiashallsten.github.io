In collaboration with artist <a href="https://www.hannaljungh.com/"
target="_blank">Hanna Ljungh</a>.

_Seismisk Händelse_ started out as a performance, and was premiered at
<a href="https://domeofvisions.com/" target="_blank">Dome of
Visions</a> in 2017. Since then the work has also been presented at
Uppsala Konstmuseum. In 2020 me and Hanna reworked the material as an
installation that was presented at _Luleåbiennalen 2020_.
