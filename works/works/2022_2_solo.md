I am adding some stuff here.

The harmonic framework used for the piece is based on the pythagorean
tuning of the shō, using the sawtooth waves to extend the <a
href="https://www.plainsound.org/pdfs/JC&ToH.pdf"
target="_blank">harmonic space</a> into seven-limit <a
href="https://marsbat.space/pdfs/JI.pdf" target="_blank">just
intonation</a>.
