Spatialised in the 29-channel speaker dome at KMH.

I composed this piece for the final presentation of my BA project,
where I explored ways of making electronic sound behave like acoustic
sound. One of the ways in which I explored this is by using feedback
and physical modeling synthesis, which is what is used in this
_Alienated II_.

These topics were thoroughly discussed in my BA thesis, titled <a
href="https://kmh.diva-portal.org/smash/record.jsf?pid=diva2:1319490"
target="_blank"><em>Rum och rundgång: Ett försök att kartlägga
ljudande upplevelser</em></a>.
