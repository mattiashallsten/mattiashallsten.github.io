This piece utilizes a synthesis algorithm where a noise source is sent
through a series of delay lines of varying, but short, delay
times. The delay lines completes a feedback loop, and each delay line
corresponds to one speaker. In a sense it is sort of like an expanded
<a
href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Karplus%E2%80%93Strong_string_synthesis"
target="_blank">karplus-strong</a> algorithm.

The piece was premiered in the 29-channel speaker dome at KMH, using 8
delay lines that were dispersed among the speakers. In the stereo
version below the delay lines are converted to a stereo signal using
the <a href="https://doc.sccode.org/Classes/Splay.html"
target="_blank">Splay</a> object in <a
href="https://supercollider.github.io/" target="_blank">SuperCollider</a>.
