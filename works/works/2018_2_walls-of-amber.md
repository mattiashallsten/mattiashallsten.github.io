Composed during the ambisonics course _Nord+MIX_ in Vilnius.

This piece is composed in <a
href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ambisonics"
target="_blank">Ambisonics</a>, with the intention of incorperating
the spatialisation in the actual synthesis. Technically, this means
that I use a SynthDef in SuperCollider that has spatialisation
parameters, allowing me to sequence not only pitches and timbre but
also spatial movement. This was partly a practical thing---I didn't
want to have to manually automate the panning in <a
href="http://reaper.fm/" target="_blank">REAPER</a>. But there is also
an aesthetic intention, where each note is independent in its
movement. Sequencing the spatialisation was also a way of introducing
chance operations in the spatialisation, with the intention of moving
away from the traditional acousmatic aesthetic of "grand gestures".
