Spatialised in the 29-channel speaker dome at KMH. The work consists
of a recording of me reading a text that discusses the new campus at
KMH, that was opened in 2016, and problematizes how the spaces of the
building are left open to the public. This is layered with field
recordings of Restaurang Oktav, the school's restaurant, during
different times of day.
