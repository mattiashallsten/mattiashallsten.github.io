---
title: Musik för speldosor
tags: mixed
desc: Modified music boxes
date: 2017
---

In this work I purchased four identical music boxes containing the
song "Happy Birthday". I then removed some of the pegs on the music
boxes, creating four distinct reductions of the melody. The four music
boxes were later attached to a wooden box, with piezo microphones
beneath each music box. The piece was then performed by four people,
each one playing a different music box. 

Premiered at Lilla Salen, Kung. Musikhögskolan 27/4 -- 2017.
